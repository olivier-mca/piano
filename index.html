Play the piano everywhere

PIANO DE VOYAGE is a lightweight, portable, modular, piano keyboard.
Made for traveling, it can fit in a suitcase or a backpack.

Portable Piano Keyboard

Connect keyboard modules to size your piano from 2 to 8 octaves

Features

Lightweight

Despite its compact size, the keys have real piano keyboard size to provide a playing experience as close as possible to regular 88-keys digital pianos. Each two-octaves keyboard module weight is less than 1kg (2.2 lbs)

Modular

Depending on the master control module plugged to your keyboard you will be able to connect to your laptop through MIDI via USB or Bluetooth (BLE) or directly connect headphones to the embedded piano sound module

The Story

"As I wanted to pratice the piano while on holidays, I started looking around for something I could put in a suitcase and travel with. Suprisingly no existing product on the market offered such a capability; there are plenty of 88 keys MIDI keyboards, but none of them can be folded or packed in a handy way. So, I decided to build my own."
- Olivier, creator of the Piano de Voyage.
