Piano de Voyage
the portable piano keyboard

Piano de Voyage is a modular, full-size, carry-on piano keyboard that will fit in your backpack

Features

Thanks to its unique assembly mechanism, the Piano de Voyage is sturdy and does not require a flat surface to lay on

Piano de Voyage offers full-size and velocity-sensitive keys

Piano de Voyage is modular, you can play with up to 4 modules of 2 octaves, the keyboard with automatically map according to the number of modules connected

USB/MIDI port (works with Mac/PC and iPhone, iPad) and connector for regular sustain pedals
