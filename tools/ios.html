Running the configuration App on iOS

As the Keyboard configuration app relies on the “Web MIDI API” which is not supported by Safari or Chrome browsers on iOS we will have to develop a specific iOS app.

Meanwhile as a workaround there is a great free app which will provide a browser that implements the required “Web MIDI API”. It is called “Web MIDI Browser“.

Install the free “Web MIDI Browser“ app :

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/web-midi-browser/id953846217

Launch the app and change the settings to allow MIDI Sysex message as follow :

Open the settings panel by clicking on the settings icon

Turn on “Enable SysEx”

Now go back to the main screen and change the URL at the top of the page to point to https://pianodevoyage.com/tools

Then the Keyboard configuration app will show up.

In order to bookmark the app URL instead of typing it each time you open the app :

Click on the “share” icon at the bottom of the screen

Then click on “Add bookmark”

Your bookmark will be added to the apps bookmarks

Now when launching the app you’ll be able to access the bookmarks by click on the “Bookmarks” icon at the bottom of the screen
